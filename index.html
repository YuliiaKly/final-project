Dice Game

Rules of Play

The user and the computer each roll a pair of dice 3 times.
Each round player and the computer will roll a pair of dice and the score for that round will be the sum of the dice.
If any of the players two dice comes up as a 1 then the score for that round for the player is 0.
If the player rolls a pair of the same numbers then the players score is the total of the two dice times 2.
The player with the highest total score at the end of the 3 rounds wins the game!
